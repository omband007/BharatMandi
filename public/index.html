🌾 Welcome to Bharat Mandi

Welcome to Bharat Mandi, your AI-powered partner in transforming farming into a thriving, digital micro-enterprise. We bridge the gap of education and digital literacy through Kisan Mitra, a voice-first assistant that speaks your native language so you can manage your farm without typing or reading. With Dr. Fasal, our instant AI diagnosis tool, you can simply "point and shoot" a photo to identify crop diseases and receive immediate chemical or organic remedies. Our Fasal-Parakh technology uses your smartphone camera to grade produce objectively, issuing a digital quality certificate that ensures you get the fair market price you deserve. By turning every farm activity into a verifiable digital record, we empower you with the data and tools needed for a dignified, profitable livelihood.
